About King David Academy

King David Academy is a Christian school located in Kanombe, dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and spiritual growth in our students. We uphold strong Christian values while preparing students for success in an ever-changing world.

Admissions

We welcome students who seek a supportive and faith-centered learning environment. Our admissions process includes an application, an interview, and assessment to ensure that each student will thrive in our community.

For application forms and detailed instructions, please contact our admissions office.

Programs

King David Academy offers internationally recognized programs to prepare students for higher education and career success:

  • International Baccalaureate (IB): A challenging and balanced curriculum that encourages critical thinking and global awareness.
  • BTEC: Practical, career-focused qualifications that provide hands-on learning and real-world skills.
